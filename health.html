TOMATO

Lycopene, Vitamin C, Potassium

Lycopene helps protect your skin and heart. Vitamin C boosts immunity and promotes a healthy glow. Potassium helps regulate body fluids and blood pressure.

CARROT

Beta-carotene, Vitamin A, Fiber

Beta-carotene turns into Vitamin A, improving eye health. Antioxidants support healthy cell growth. Insoluble Fiber helps digestion and gives a feeling of fullness.

BROCCOLI

Vitamin C, Vitamin K, Fiber, Iron

Vitamin C strengthens your immune system. Vitamin K supports bone health. Fiber promotes digestion and keeps you full longer. Iron helps deliver oxygen throughout your body.

ONION

Quercetin, Vitamin B6, Antioxidants

Quercetin supports heart health and reduces inflammation. Vitamin B6 helps energy metabolism. Antioxidants strengthen your immune system.
